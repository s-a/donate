Why this page?

In the recent years several tools, libs and plugins for web development have been developed. We hope you will find these tools helpful for your work. They are all free for commercial and non-commercial projects and available without any restrictions at all. However, if you really work with one of them often, please think about donating (options below). For discussions and questions you can contact us at the regarding issue section at GitHub. Our official collection of repositories can be found there.
